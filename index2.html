i have a little present for you 🎁

At first, we were just two strangers who met by chance.
I never expected that the little acts of care you gave me would make you become the person I wait
for every single day.

Little by little, we grew closer.
I became familiar with your laugh, your tears, the way you get sad,
the way you get angry, the way you tell your stories — even the smallest things about you can make me smile.

I was enchanted to meet you.

You are beautiful, kind, caring, mature… to me, you are perfect.

The longer I get to know you, the more certain I become that my feelings are no longer just admiration.
You have become someone truly important in my life.
I don’t want to lose you.

If you ever ask me what made me fall in love with you,
it’s because whenever I’m with you, my life feels more beautiful and more colorful.

💌 Untuk Sylvia
